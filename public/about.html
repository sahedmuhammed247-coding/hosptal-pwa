Hospital Appointment Booking System

The Hospital Appointment Booking System is a Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed to make booking doctor appointments simple, fast, and convenient.

Features

  • ✅ Patient Registration
  • ✅ Patient Login
  • ✅ Hospital Selection
  • ✅ Doctor Selection
  • ✅ Online Appointment Booking
  • ✅ Appointment Confirmation
  • ✅ My Appointments
  • ✅ Hospital Admin Dashboard
  • ✅ Progressive Web App (PWA) Support

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce waiting time in hospitals and provide patients with an easy and secure online appointment booking experience.
